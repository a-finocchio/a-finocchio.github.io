About Me

a   technophile

Hi, I am Huixiang, a robotics software engineer.

I recently completed a master's in MechE Robotics at Johns Hopkins University. Two years of study provides me a good overview in the robotics field, and I am greatly interested in SLAM, motion planning and robot perception. For my future career, I’d love to keep working on some of these robotics topics. Though far away from the top, I won't quit climbing on this mountain.

I received my first B.S degree at Purdue University (IUPUI), majoring in Mechanical Engineering, and my second B.S degree at Sun Yat-sen University, majoring in Theoretical and Applied Mechanics. Thanks to the intensive academic training during my undergraduate’s study, I established solid engineering foundations in Mechanical Engineering and Mechanics.

Last year, I interned at Hai Robotics, a company that develop logistics robots for warehouse automation. Whilst there, I assisted in refining their products and worked on challenging research projects. Though four-months period is short, I really appreciate the experience of challenging myself and learning from great colleague.

"The palest ink is better than the best memory". In the future, I’d love to share my robotics experience and learning notes...
